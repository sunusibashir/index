engine removal

-important steps
  1. disconnect battery cables
  2. remove the hood
  3. remove air clener
  4. label all wire and vacuum line
  5. drain coolant and oil
  6. remove the radiater
  7. remove the dirstributor and spark plug wiring
  8. remove the direct current(DC) generator
  9. remove the heater hoses and ground strap
